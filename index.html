About Me

Introduction and Personal Summary

Hello! My name is Bukeka Nxumalo, and I am a passionate Full Stack Web Developer with a knack for creating innovative and user-friendly websites. My journey into the world of technology began in high school, where I developed a keen interest in computers and programming. This passion led me to pursue a degree in Information Technology at Durban University of Technology, where I have been expanding my knowledge and skills in various areas of web development.

Throughout my university studies, I have gained a solid foundation in programming languages such as HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and C#, as well as frameworks like ASP.NET and the .NET Framework. My coursework and projects have provided me with hands-on experience in developing web applications, understanding database management, and implementing best practices in software development.

Additionally, I have actively participated in university tech clubs, which have further enhanced my problem-solving abilities and teamwork skills. My academic journey has been complemented by self-initiated projects and continuous learning through online courses and tutorials, enabling me to stay updated with the latest trends and technologies in web development.
